Dazed & Confused

1993 ‧ Comedy/Indie film ‧ 1h 43m ‧ Director: Richard Linklater

1976 Texas, a bunch of teenagers celebrate the last day of high school by
hazing freshmen, partying at a local pool, indulging in drugs
and following it up with a keg party



Scott Pilgrim vs. The World

2010 ‧ Action/Comedy ‧ 1h 52m ‧ Director: Edgar Wright

Scott Pilgrim meets Ramona and instantly falls in love with her.
But when he meets one of her exes at a band competition,
he realizes that he has to deal with all seven of her exes to woo her.



Dead Poets Society

1989 ‧ Drama/Teen ‧ 2h 20m ‧ Director: Peter Weir

John Keating, a progressive English teacher, tries to encourage his
students to break free from the norm, go against the status quo
and live life unapologetically.



10 Things I Hate About You

1999 ‧ Romance/Comedy ‧ 1h 39m ‧ Director: Gil Junger

A high-school boy, Cameron, cannot date Bianca until her
anti-social older sister, Kat, has a boyfriend. So, Cameron pays a
mysterious boy, Patrick, to charm Kat.



Mid90s

2018 ‧ Comedy/Drama ‧ 1h 25m ‧ Director: Jonah Hill

In 1990s Los Angeles, a 13-year-old spends his summer navigating
between a troubled home life and a crew of new friends
he meets at a skate shop.



David Attenborough: A life on our plantet

2020 ‧ Documentary ‧ 1h 54m ‧ Directors: Jonathan Hughes, Keith Scholey, Alastair Fothergill, Jonnie Hughes

In his 93 years, Attenborough has visited every continent on the globe,
exploring the wild places of the planet and documenting the living world in all
its variety and wonder. But during his lifetime, Attenborough has also seen
first-hand the monumental scale of humanity's impact on nature.



Life Itself

2018 ‧ Drama/Comedy ‧ 1h 58m ‧ Director: Dan Fogelman

As a couple from New York prepares to bring their first child into the world,
the twists of their journey give rise to a chain of events that affects the life of
another family across the ocean.



Perks of being a wallflower

2012 ‧ Romance/Drama ‧ 1h 45m ‧ Director: Stephen Chbosky

Charlie, a 15-year-old introvert, enters high school and is nervous about his new life.
When he befriends his seniors, he learns to cope with his friend's suicide and his tumultuous past.



Juno

2007 ‧ Romance/Comedy ‧ 1h 36m ‧ Director: Jason Reitman

Social misfit Juno protects herself with a caustic wit, but her unplanned
pregnancy has the teen getting more involved in the lives of her
baby's adoptive parents than she expected.



Big Fish

2003 ‧ Drama/Fantasy ‧ 2h 5m ‧ Director: Tim Burton

Will Bloom returns home to care for his dying father, who had a penchant for telling unbelievable stories.
After he passes away, Will tries to find out if his tales were really true.
